Nature Resort

There are also a lot of nature resorts that can be so amazing in the naked eyes. Tourist who loves. Nature tend to enjoy this type of places. CALABARZON is recommended for nature lovers. Discover the best top things to do in the Calabarzon Region of the Philippines, such as Minasawa Bird Sanctuary, Quezon National Forest Park, Bangkong Kahoy Valley, Mount Purro Nature Reserve, Masungi Georeserve, Dalitiwan River, Hidden Valley Springs, Grand Villa Resort Butterfly Center, Bluroze Farms, and Carasacos Falls.
