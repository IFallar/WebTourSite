Hot Spring

Different types of springs that you can enjoy here in CALABARZON. The tourist who wants to relax may need to visit some hot springs here. If there is one thing that distinguishes Laguna from other Philippine tourist spots, it is its proximity to a variety of hot spring resorts. Many visitors are drawn to its natural hot springs, which are not only soothing to the senses but are also thought to ease tension and muscle discomfort. Because of the geothermal heat and steam released by nearby Mount Makiling, a dormant volcano bordered by Laguna and Batangas, this province in Southern Luzon is rich in hot springs. The majority of these hot springs are located in Pansol, Los Baos, and Calamba City.
