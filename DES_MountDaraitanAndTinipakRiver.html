MOUNT DARAITAN AND TINIPAK RIVER

Mt. Daraitan is a jewel in the heart of the Sierra Madre mountain range, near the border of Tanay, Rizal, and General Nakar, Quezon. It provides a tranquil view of the sea of clouds, sunrise, the Daraitan River, and the Sierra Madre Mountain range. It is located in Tanay, Rizal’s Barangay Daraitan. Mt. Daraitan stands 739 meters or 2,424 feet above sea level. Mt. Daraitan is a popular mountain in Tanay, Rizal, which is a first-class municipality. From clean rivers and beautiful caves that are approximately a hundred years old structure of pillar and pebble rocks to the delightful view of the Sierra Madre mountain range and the beautiful Tinipak River.
