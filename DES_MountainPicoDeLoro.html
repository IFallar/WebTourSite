MOUNT PICO DE LORO

Mt. Pico de Loro, also known as Mt. Palay Palay, towers majestically between Maragondon, Cavite, and Nasugbu, Batangas. It is a dormant volcano rising over 664 meters above sea level. It is regarded as one of the best hiking destinations in the Philippines. The name “Pico de Loro” refers to a parrot’s beak that resembles the mountain’s summit. Its stunning summit provides an unobstructed view of the neighboring provinces of Laguna and Batangas. If you are into hiking then this destination is highly recommended for you.
