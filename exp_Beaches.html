Beaches

Calabarzon also has a lot of white beaches that a lot of tourists can enjoy. You will enjoy a wonderful scenery that can capture your heart when you experience it. Calabarzon offers some beautiful ocean views. That is why this area is excellent for Tourist interested in diving. Explore the marine riches of Anilao in Batangas, known as the nudibranch capital of the Philippines. Learn how to get there, where to dive, and what kind of species to expect in our Anilao diving guide. The province of Batangas, which is part of the Calabarzon region, is located in the southwest corner of the island of Luzon. It is well-known for tourist attractions such as the Taal Volcano, Taal Heritage Town (which contains ancestral structures from the nineteenth century), beaches, and dive spots in Mabini, Tingloy, Bauan, Nasugbu, Calatagan, and San Juan.
