Taal Heritage Village

The most intriguing part of walking about Taal town proper is seeing the heritage houses, which allow visitors to journey back in time because their particular exquisiteness from the past has been preserved for the present generation to view. Some of these heritage houses were converted into museums, others into galleries, and a few into gift shops. However, in general, these heritage residences serve as tangible reminders of the rich cultural history passed down from generation to generation. The Heritage Village will allow visitors to experience Taaleos' colorful way of life in the past.
