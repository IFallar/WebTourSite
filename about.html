ABOUT US
Webtour: CALABARZON is a website for travellers, vacationers, and people who likes to discover the beauty that lies in CALABARZON. It features the provinces of Cavite, Laguna, Batangas, Rizal, and Quezon. The visitors of the website can gain information about the different destinations in CALABARZON as well as what the people experience while visiting it's tourist attractions. The purpose of this website is really to show the destinations and tourist spots of CALABARZON and give them inspiration to visit those places.
