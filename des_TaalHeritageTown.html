TAAL HERITAGE TOWN

The most interesting aspect of walking around Taal town proper is seeing the heritage houses, which allow people to travel back in time because their unique exquisiteness from the past has been preserved for the present generation to see. Some of these heritage houses were converted into museums, others into galleries, and a few into gift shops. However, in general, these heritage houses serve as tangible reminders of the rich cultural heritage passed down from generation to generation. The Heritage Village will allow visitors to experience Taaleos’ colorful way of life in the past.
