Espasol

Espasol is a soft, chewy Filipino rice cake made with toasted glutinous rice flour and grated green coconut cooked slowly in coconut milk. Before coating each cake in rice flour, the mixture is transformed into a soft, pliable dough that is shaped into long cylinders or triangles. Espasol is associated with the Laguna region, where it is regarded as a regional specialty that can be purchased at a variety of restaurants, stores, and street stalls.
