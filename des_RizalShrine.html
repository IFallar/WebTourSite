RIZAL SHRINE

This is Dapitan City’s most important historical landmark. It refers to Dr. Jose P. Rizal’s approximately 16-hectare estate in Barangay Talisay, which he purchased with his lottery winnings. The landmark was intended to be a place of punishment, but instead it has become an icon of inspiration for many generations who come to see the national hero’s ingenuity during his days. The shrine contains a collection of structures that retell the stories of former Dapitans as well as the passion of an extraordinary man who lived as an exile with a purpose—to teach and serve the Dapitanons. The stories told there, the poems written, and the insights gained are the legacy of his life.
